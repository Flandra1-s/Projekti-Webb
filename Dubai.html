Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about Dubai.
Let us help make your journey unforgettable.

What is the Best Time to Visit Dubai?

+

The best time to visit Dubai is between November and March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities.

Do I Need a Visa to Visit Dubai?

+

Visitors from many countries can obtain a visa on arrival. Please check with your local UAE embassy for details.

What is the Currency in Dubai?

+

The currency in Dubai is the UAE Dirham (AED). Credit cards are widely accepted, but cash is recommended for small transactions.
