Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about traveling to Beijing, China.

What is the Best Time to Visit Beijing?

+

The best time to visit Beijing is in the spring (April to May) or autumn (September to October) for pleasant weather.

Do I Need a Visa to Visit Beijing?

+

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Check with your local Chinese consulate for accurate details.

What is the Currency in Beijing?

+

The currency in Beijing is the Chinese Yuan (CNY).
