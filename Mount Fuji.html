What is the Best Time to Visit Japan?

+

The best time to visit is during spring (March-May) for cherry blossoms or autumn (September-November) for vibrant foliage.

Do I Need a Rail Pass?

+

A Japan Rail Pass is highly recommended for long-distance travel, offering unlimited rides on trains across Japan.
