Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about traveling to Spain.

What is the Best Time to Visit Spain?

+

The best time to visit Spain is in the spring (April to June) or fall (September to October) for pleasant weather and fewer tourists.

Do I Need a Visa to Visit Spain?

+

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Check with your local Spanish consulate for accurate details.

What is the Currency in Spain?

+

The currency in Spain is the Euro (EUR).
