Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about Malaysia.
Let us help make your journey unforgettable.

What is the Best Time to Visit Malaysia?

+

The best time to visit Malaysia is between March and October, avoiding the rainy season.

Do I Need a Visa to Visit Malaysia?

+

Many nationalities can enter Malaysia visa-free for short stays. Check your country's requirements.

What is the Currency in Malaysia?

+

The currency in Malaysia is the Malaysian Ringgit (MYR). Credit cards are widely accepted, but cash is useful for smaller transactions.
